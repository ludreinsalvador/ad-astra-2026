Privacy Notice

De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde (DLS-CSB) respects your right to privacy and is committed to protect

our control. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde (DLS-CSB) shall only retain the said personal information until it serves its purpose, after which it shall be securely disposed of.
